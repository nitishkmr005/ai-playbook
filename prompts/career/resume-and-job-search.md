# Resume & Job Search Prompts

Four prompts to rebuild, optimize, and tailor your resume — and one to clarify any project before starting.

Source: @yourchatgptguide

---

## 1. The Complete Resume Rebuild

Full rewrite: converts responsibilities into achievements, strengthens language, makes it ATS-friendly.

```
Act as a top-tier HR hiring expert who has reviewed over 100,000 resumes. Rewrite my resume to make it ATS-friendly, achievement-focused, and irresistible to recruiters. Convert every responsibility into measurable achievements using numbers, results, and impact. Strengthen weak bullet points with powerful action verbs and quantify success wherever possible.

Here is my current resume: [PASTE RESUME]
The job I am applying for is: [INSERT ROLE]
```

**Variables:** `[RESUME]`, `[ROLE]`

---

## 2. Job-Specific Resume Optimizer

Matches your resume to a specific job description — maximizes ATS score for that posting.

```
Take this job description: [PASTE JOB DESCRIPTION] and match my resume to it by rewriting bullet points, rearranging sections, inserting relevant keywords, and aligning my experience to the role's requirements. Make sure it looks like the perfect match so the ATS ranks me in the top 3 candidates.

Here is my resume: [PASTE RESUME]
```

**Variables:** `[JOB DESCRIPTION]`, `[RESUME]`

---

## 3. The Recruiter Mind-Reader

Rewrites your summary section so a hiring manager wants to call you in the first 5 seconds.

```
Pretend you are the hiring manager for the position [INSERT JOB TITLE]. Rewrite my resume summary so that in 5 seconds you instantly want to interview me. Focus on results, credibility, uniqueness, and proof of performance.

Here is my current summary: [PASTE SUMMARY]
My career highlights: [PASTE HIGHLIGHTS]
```

**Variables:** `[JOB TITLE]`, `[CURRENT SUMMARY]`, `[CAREER HIGHLIGHTS]`

---

## 4. ATS Keyword Injection

Extracts the exact keywords from a job description and inserts them naturally so you pass automated screening.

```
Extract the most important skills, keywords, and technical language from this job description: [PASTE JD]. Insert them naturally into my resume so it passes the ATS scan with a match score of 95% or higher. Then show me which keywords you added and why.
```

**Variables:** `[JOB DESCRIPTION]`

---

## 5. The Project Clarification Interview

Use before any significant project — interview yourself through the AI to surface what you actually want.

Source: Wyndo (1.3K likes)

```
I'm about to start this project. Interview me until you have 95% confidence about what I actually want, not what I think I should want.
```

**Why it works:** The gap between what you say you want and what you actually want is where most failed projects begin. AI asking YOU questions is 10x more powerful than you asking AI questions.
