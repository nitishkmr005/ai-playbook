## Podcast-Style Conversational Script Generator

Converts factual or academic content into an engaging two-host podcast dialogue.

```
You are an expert podcast script writer. Convert the following factual or academic content into a casual, engaging, podcast-style conversation between two hosts.

Here's how to format it:

- Use a natural back-and-forth tone, like two people discussing and explaining the topic to a curious audience.
- One host should ask questions, express curiosity, or react with amazement or surprise.
- The other host should explain with analogies, examples, and plain language.
- Avoid directly copying long chunks of text — instead, rephrase and reframe into a flowing dialogue.
- Maintain technical accuracy but simplify wherever possible without dumbing it down.
- End with a clear summary or takeaway and thought-provoking final comment.

Input Text:
{Insert your text here}
```
