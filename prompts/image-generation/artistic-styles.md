# Artistic Style Prompts

Templates for applying distinct artistic styles to any subject.

---

## Charcoal Sketch

> A charcoal sketch of a raven in flight, raw and textured with expressive shading and bold linework. The background is filled with smudged gradients and sketchbook marks, giving it a gritty, unrefined elegance.

**Template:** Replace `raven in flight` with any subject.
**Tags:** `charcoal`, `sketch`, `textured`, `monochrome`

---

## Industrial Blueprint / Patent Drawing

> A blueprint schematic of a [subject], drawn in the style of early 20th-century industrial patents. Rendered in crisp blue ink with white technical lines, featuring exploded views, angular labels, and stamped diagram codes.

**Usage:** Replace `[subject]` with any object or machine.
**Tags:** `blueprint`, `technical`, `vintage`, `patent`, `template`

---

## Ancient Egyptian Papyrus Diagram

> An ancient Egyptian papyrus diagram of a [subject], composed of flat, symbolic representations using traditional side profiles. Painted in earthy ochre, turquoise, and charcoal with hieroglyphic notations.

**Usage:** Replace `[subject]` with any object or figure.
**Tags:** `egyptian`, `papyrus`, `ancient`, `symbolic`, `template`

## Pixar / Clay Restyle (from photo)

> Restyle this image to a 3D render, smooth texture and a soft skin, large expressive eyes, cartoonish, soft lighting, subtle glow, highly detailed with a Pixar-like style.

**Note:** Requires a reference photo.
**Tags:** `pixar`, `3d`, `cartoon`, `restyle`, `portrait`

---

## 3D Character from Photo (Clay Style)

> Turn the real photo on the right into a stylized 3D character illustration like the character in the left image. Smooth, clay-like textures. Soft lighting with a subtle glow. Friendly and cartoonish expression. High attention to detail and personality.

**Note:** Requires two images — a style reference (left) and a subject photo (right).
**Tags:** `3d`, `clay`, `character`, `portrait`, `restyle`

## Toy Action Figure

> Create a toy of the person in the photo. Let it be an action figure. Next to the figure, there should be the toy's equipment like flowers, garden, and pen. Also, on top of the box, write 'Lalbhagh Flower Show'. Visualize this in a realistic way.

**Note:** Requires a reference photo. Customize box text and accessories to fit the subject.
**Tags:** `toy`, `action-figure`, `portrait`, `product`, `realistic`
