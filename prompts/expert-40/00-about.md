## How to Use

1. **Save this collection.** You won't memorize 40 prompts — don't try.
2. **Pick 5** most relevant to your work this week. Copy, customize the variables, use them.
3. **When a prompt produces excellent results**, save the customized version to your own template file. Over time you build a personal library.
4. **Every week, try 2–3 prompts** from categories you haven't used before. Expand gradually.

The compounding effect: after one month of consistent use, your AI workflow operates at a level most people won't reach for years.
